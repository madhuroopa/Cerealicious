In this visualization, we can analyze the nutrition proportions for different milk types and cereals. For milk types, the serving size is 240 ml. For Cereal types, the serving size is around 40 grams. By calculating the proportions of carbs, fiber, protein, etc in relation to the sum of serving sizes of respective milk or cereal types, we obtain a standardized measure that allows us to compare the relative contribution of these nutrients across different cereals and milk types.
